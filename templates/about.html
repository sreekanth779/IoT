ABOUT US

Welcome to our motion classification project! We are a team passionate about leveraging technology to improve motion classification and enhance data-driven decision-making processes.


Our Mission

Our mission is to develop a robust motion classification system using Raspberry Pi Pico accelerometers and GPS sensors. By combining real-time sensor data with advanced analytics, we aim to provide insights into motion patterns and behaviors for various applications, including transportation, sports analytics, and health monitoring.

Project Highlights

Utilizing Raspberry Pi Pico accelerometers and GPS sensors for data collection.,
Implementing real-time data processing and visualization with Django and Charts.
Developing motion classification algorithms for predictive analytics.
Building an interactive dashboard for monitoring and analyzing motion data.
